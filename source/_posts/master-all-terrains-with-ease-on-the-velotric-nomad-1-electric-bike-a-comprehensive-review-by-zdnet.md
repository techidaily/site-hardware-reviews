## 1. Grill-Equipped Electric Bike: Get Your Ride and BBQ Set in One![ZDNet Review]

![A man in camouflage and wearing a hiking backpack on a Bakcou eBike, pedaling through a woodland clearing](https://www.zdnet.com/a/img/resize/9a1c1acc8230365f824d026833de95ed6b4b0740/2024/06/14/1df8d2ec-cc81-43ce-b185-6f1d7f03ef17/bakcou-cover-image.jpg?auto=webp&width=1280)

Bakcou/ZDNET

Got an outdoorsy dad that's hard to buy for? Looking to pick up an eBike and like free things? [Bakcou](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fpages%2Ffathers-day-24) is offering a 28-inch Blackstone Omnivore Griddle propane grill (a $350 value) for free with the purchase of any new eBike from their lineup.

[Get a free Blackstone Omnivore with purchase (a $350 value) View now at Bakcou](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fpages%2Ffathers-day-24)

The [Flatlander](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fproducts%2Fflatlander-fat-tire-electric-bike) series is designed for for mountain trail riders and hunters looking to travel light. You can configure it with up to a 25 amp hour battery and either a 29.5 or 24-inch tall frame. With a top speed of 25 mph and a maximum range of up to 80 miles, it's perfect for hitting park trails and hunting trails. 

The [Kodiak](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fpages%2Fkodiak) eBike from Bakcou is built for more experienced trail riders and outdoor enthusiasts, allowing you to easily switch between rear, front, and all-wheel drive for tackling almost any type of terrain. The 25Ah battery gives you a top speed of 35 mph and a max range of 80 miles. 

The [Mule](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fproducts%2Fmule-fat-tire-electric-bike) is Bakcou's workhorse model, designed for everything from trail riding and hunting to city commutes and leisurely rides. The 25Ah battery provides a top speed of 35mph and a max range of 80 miles. The Mule also has an ultra-tough [Jäger version](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fproducts%2Fmule-jager), with a 14-speed, all-metal gearbox and "smart motor" with sensors that monitor speed, braking, and even torque to prevent the motor from overloading and provide a smooth riding experience. 

The [Scout](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fproducts%2Fscout-fat-tire-electric-bike) is a full-suspension eBike specifically for mountain trail riding. It's built with wide tires for better traction on dirt and gravel while the 11-speed gearbox gives you total control over your bike. The bike's suspension system was designed with rough terrain in mind, letting you tackle off-road trails and landscapes either under pedal power or with the electric motor assist. You can configure the bike with up to a 21Ah battery for a top speed of 35mph and a max range of 65 miles; it also has a [Jäger model](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fproducts%2Fscout-jager) with a 14-speed gearbox and integrated torque sensors. 

The [Storm](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fproducts%2Fstorm-fat-tire-electric-bike) is another full-suspension eBike from Bakcou designed for tough mountain terrain. Experienced riders can take advantage of the Bafang Ultra mid-drive motor with its all-metal construction and integrated torque sensors for up to 1500 watts of output and high-thrills mountain biking. You can configure the bike with up to a 25Ah battery for a maximum speed of 35mph and a range of 80 miles. The [Jäger version](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fproducts%2Fstorm-jager-fat-tire-electric-bike) uses a German-engineered Rohloff hub, which features an internal gear system rather than a traditional cassette for smoother shifting and less risk of chain jumping while out riding. 

Bakcou's [Mav3](https://www.avantlink.com/click.php?tt=app&ti=1019&mi=25573&pw=262625&ctc=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp&url=https%3A%2F%2Fbakcou.com%2Fproducts%2Fmav3) eBike has been designed specifically for use by police, military, and first responders who may need a lightweight, maneuverable vehicle to access remote areas during natural disasters or emergencies. The 30Ah battery gives you a maximum range of 90 miles and a top speed of 35mph for faster response times then every second matters. The Bafang Ultra M620 gearbox features an internal shift design for better reliability and smoother shifting; It also allows you to switch between 750W and 1000W outputs to be Class 1-compliant when responders need to traverse both off-road and on streets. 

## When will this deal expire?

You're going to want to hurry with this deal: Bakcou is only offering the Blackstone Omnivore griddle grill with the purchase of a new eBike until June 17.
