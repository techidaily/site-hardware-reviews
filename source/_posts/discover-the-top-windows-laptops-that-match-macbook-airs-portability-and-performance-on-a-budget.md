## Discover the New Era of High-Performance Gaming with Nvidia's Cutting-Edge Studio Laptops and Superior GPU Power - Detailed Insights

![Nvidia new laptop showcased on a green and grey striped background](https://www.zdnet.com/a/img/resize/233e3b96c9b91467f8f498da6277aa5df9f5d9a6/2023/01/03/8542f9a4-84ec-4591-8adc-5346c0d75857/screen-shot-2023-01-03-at-1-29-24-pm.png?auto=webp&width=1280)

On Tuesday at CES, [Nvidia announced](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/geforce/news/geforce-rtx-40-series-laptops-available-february-8/) a new series of laptops that feature its fastest, most capable graphics processing units (GPUs). Starting in February, the GeForce RTX 40 Series GPUs are going to be found in Studio Laptops, making the laptops three times more efficient than previous generation flagship models. The laptops will be available from several manufacturers, including Acer, Alienware, ASUS, Dell, Gigabyte, HP, Lenovo, MSI, Razer, and Samsung. 

**Also:** [**The best gaming laptops**](https://www.zdnet.com/article/best-gaming-laptop/)

The GeForce RTX 40 Series Studio Laptops are meant to bring portable gaming to new heights and unleash "a new era of laptop computing," according to Nvidia. The laptops exhibit a thin, portable design without compromising speed, battery life, graphics or efficiency. 

![geforce rtx 40 series laptops on a black and green background](https://www.zdnet.com/a/img/resize/eced57be172021c4448ad09e197c1b732477fa4c/2023/01/03/218fa790-6be9-406f-a977-2e02699468ef/geforce-rtx-40-series-laptops.jpg?auto=webp&width=1280)

GeForce RTX 40 Series Laptops.

Image: Nvidia

The laptops are powered by Nvidia's Ada Lovelace architecture, GeForce RTX 40 Series Laptop GPUs, and new 5th generation Max-Q technologies, which provide users with up to 4x faster performance, previously impossible detail, massive speedups in 3D, video and broadcast workflows, and AI tools.
