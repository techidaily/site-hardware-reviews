## Switching Your Primary Mobile Phone Application in Android

With everything that phones can do, it's easy to forget they are phones at the core. On Android, you have the choice to swap your phone (dialer) apps with something else from the Play Store. We'll show you how to do it.

[You don't have to use the stock apps](https://youtube-zero.techidaily.com/ed-in-2024-essential-guide-to-hashtagging-for-game-focused-yt-channels/) that came with your phone. [Google has a phone app](https://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/3607085/type/dlg/sid/UUhtgUeUpU206229/https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.dialer) that can be installed from the Play Store on most Android phones. There are other third-party options as well.

 If you'd like to try one of these, you'll need to set it as the "default" phone app after installing it from the Play Store.

 First, swipe down once or twice from the top of the screen---depending on your phone---to open the notification shade and tap the gear icon.
